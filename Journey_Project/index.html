Description

Journey is a Health and Wellness mobile application that serves three main purposes. The first purpose is to document a User’s fitness and nutrition lifestyle on a granular level through photographs. The second purpose of this application is to allow other Users of the application, such as health and wellness professionals, nutritionists and Doctors to understand their client’s every day habits and systems. The third main purpose of LEAD is to provide a social media platform for individuals to ‘lead by example' in the health and wellness community.
